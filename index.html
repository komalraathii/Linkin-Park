Linkin Park

Linkin Park is an American rock band formed in 1996 in Agoura Hills, California. Their music blends metal, rap rock, and alternative styles. The band gained worldwide fame with their debut album, Hybrid Theory, The band became popular due to michael bay, the director of transformers, as What I've done was used in transformers end credits.

Albums

Some of their famous albums include:

  • Hybrid Theory
  • Meteora
  • Minutes to Midnight
  • One More Light
